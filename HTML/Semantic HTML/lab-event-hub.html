Pohela Boishakh

Pohela Boishakh is the Bengali New Year celebrated by the Bengali people worldwide and as a holiday on 14 April in Bangladesh

Victory Day

Victory Day in Bangladesh, celebrated on December 16th, commemorates the nation's independence in 1971 and is marked with a 31-gun salute, wreath-laying at memorials, hoisting national flags, and special programs.
