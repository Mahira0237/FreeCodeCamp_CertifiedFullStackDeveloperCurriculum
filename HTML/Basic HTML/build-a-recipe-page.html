Mushroom Soup

Recipe of a bowl of delicious mushroom soup

Ingredients

  • Mushroom
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Chicken Stock

Instructions

  1. Sautee the musrooms in butter
  2. Add flour and make roux
  3. Add milk in intervals
  4. Add stock cute when the milk starts boiling
  5. Stir till the soup comes to your desired thickness and serve
